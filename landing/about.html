A solution for small businesses

We will help small businesses in towns with an online presence and easy-to-use interface, helping the process in which users search for their favorite products or services on a single platform. Businesses will facilitate migration to the digital age so that they can take advantage of all its benefits.
Initially, Haana will focus on restaurants, but we intend to expand this project to other types of commerce.

Search stores by town

Search all the stores in your town with their own description, and phones to keep in touch with them if you want to have more details

Detailed information

Haana bring you detailed info about each commerce so you can take the best commerce to supplies your needs.

Product catalog

If you aren't sure about what services the commerce offers, you can see its products with detailed info before contacting the commerce.

Why Haana?

In non-capital cities there is still a communication barrier between service providers and their potential users due to the costs and economic and technical difficulties of having a website where it would be easy to present and update its content: in addition to the cost of its publication and positioning.

Haana will solve the gap presented between the commerces and users uploading store information to the internet to have an easy communication between them.
